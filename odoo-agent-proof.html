Business Problem

Product and stock knowledge changes faster than a sales team can memorize it.

  • Sales answers depend on product availability, variants, and stock status.
  • Training time increases when product knowledge sits in people's heads.
  • Customers need clearer access to product data before paying or ordering.

Technical Shape

A live-data path keeps the answer grounded in the source system.

  • Sales assistant receives a product, stock, price, or symptom-based question.
  • Read-only Odoo lookup returns product, category, stock, and availability fields.
  • Customer-facing search can expose useful product status while private fields stay protected.

Agent Lookup Flow

1. Ask Can we recommend a low-stock industrial filter for a customer who needs replacement this week?
2. Fetch Odoo API lookup by SKU, OEM code, category, or parsed customer intent returns product facts and stock caveats.
3. Answer Sales gets a grounded answer plus caveats: missing price, low stock, out of stock, or confirm final quantity before quote.
